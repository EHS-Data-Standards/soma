    Class: OxidativeStressAssay

    Assay for measuring oxidative stress markers. Informs on Key Event: "Increased oxidative stress" - often a Molecular Initiating Event (MIE) in respiratory toxicology AOPs.

    TIER 1 slots (critical): All surrogate markers for oxidative stress. Multiple detection methods expected - no single "best" for lung function.
